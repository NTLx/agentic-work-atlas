# Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) - NYC

Careers

Model Deployment for Business - New York City

## About the team

OpenAI's Forward Deployed Engineering team partners with customers to turn research breakthroughs into production systems. It operates at the intersection of customer delivery and core platform development.

## About the role

Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) lead complex end-to-end deployments of frontier models in production alongside OpenAI's most strategic customers. The role owns discovery, technical scoping, system design, build, and production rollout, partnering directly with customer engineering and domain teams.

Success is measured through production adoption, measurable workflow impact, and eval-driven feedback that changes product and model roadmaps. The role works closely with Product, Research, Partnerships, GRC, Security, and GTM teams.

This role is based in NYC. OpenAI uses a hybrid work model of 3 days in the office per week, offers relocation assistance, and notes that travel up to 50% is required.

## In this role you will

- Own technical delivery across multiple deployments from first prototype to stable production.
- Build full-stack systems that deliver customer value and sharpen how the team learns.
- Embed closely with customer teams, understand their needs, and guide adoption of what you build.
- Scope work, sequence delivery, and remove blockers early.
- Make trade-offs between scope, speed, and quality, and adjust plans to protect delivery.
- Contribute directly in the code when progress or clarity depends on it.
- Codify working patterns into tools, playbooks, or building blocks that others can use.
- Share field feedback that helps Research and Product understand where the models succeed and where they can improve.
- Keep teams moving through clarity and follow-through.

## You might thrive in this role if you

- Bring 5+ years of engineering or technical deployment experience that includes customer-facing work.
- Have scoped and delivered complex systems in fast-moving or ambiguous environments.
- Write and review production-grade code across frontend and backend using Python, JavaScript, or comparable stacks.
- Have built or deployed systems powered by LLMs or generative models and understand how model behavior affects product experience.
- Simplify complexity and make fast, sound decisions under pressure.
- Communicate clearly with engineers, product teams, and customer stakeholders.
- Spot risks early and adjust without slowing down.
- Model calm and judgment when the stakes are high.

## Compensation

USD 162K - USD 280K + offers equity.

## 编译摘要

### 1. 浓缩
- **核心结论1**: OpenAI 官方职位页把 FDE 定义成一条端到端部署闭环，而不是单点技术支持岗位。
  - 关键证据: 职责从 `discovery`、`technical scoping`、`system design` 一直延伸到 `build` 和 `production rollout`。
- **核心结论2**: FDE 的成功标准已经从“是否交付代码”转向“是否被组织采用并产生业务结果”。
  - 关键证据: 官方明确用 `production adoption`、`measurable workflow impact` 和 `eval-driven feedback` 衡量成功。
- **核心结论3**: 这是一个横跨客户现场、产品、研究、治理与安全的高模糊度角色。
  - 关键证据: 职位页列出与 customer engineering、domain teams、Product、Research、Partnerships、GRC、Security、GTM 等多方协作，并要求在高压和高不确定性中持续推进。

### 2. 质疑
- **关于“端到端闭环”的质疑**: 这是岗位理想画像，真实组织里 discovery、system design、delivery、adoption 往往被多角色分摊，个人是否真能闭环取决于组织授权。
- **关于“workflow impact 可度量”的质疑**: 职位页强调结果导向，但并没有给出统一度量框架；不同客户、不同流程下的 impact 口径可能高度不一致。
- **关于“能力要求”的质疑**: 该角色要求同时具备工程、客户沟通、模型理解、风险判断和交付推进，门槛极高，天然限制了供给规模。
